Course Overview

Sadhan Panchakam, or "Five Spiritual Practices," is a text that outlines the essential practices for spiritual growth. This course offers a deep exploration of these practices.

Course Features

  • In-depth study of each spiritual practice
  • Practical exercises and meditation techniques
  • Guidance on integrating practices into daily life
  • Group discussions and experience sharing
  • Personal mentoring sessions
