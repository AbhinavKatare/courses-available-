Vedanta Saar Course

Welcome to the Pragya Trust Vedanta Saar Course. Explore the essence of Vedanta philosophy and its practical applications in daily life.

Course Overview

Vedanta Saar, or "The Essence of Vedanta," distills the core teachings of Vedantic philosophy. This course offers a comprehensive study of these fundamental principles.

Course Features

  • In-depth exploration of key Vedantic concepts
  • Study of important Vedantic texts and commentaries
  • Guided meditation and self-inquiry practices
  • Interactive discussions and Q&A sessions
  • Practical exercises for applying Vedanta in daily life
