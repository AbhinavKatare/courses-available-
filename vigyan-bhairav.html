Vigyan Bhairav Course

Welcome to the Pragya Trust Vigyan Bhairav Course. Explore the ancient tantric text, Vigyan Bhairav Tantra, which provides 112 techniques for meditation and self-realization.

Course Overview

The Vigyan Bhairav Tantra is a profound text that offers a wide array of meditation techniques. This course provides an in-depth exploration of these practices and their applications in modern life.

Course Features

  • Detailed study of the 112 meditation techniques
  • Practical guidance on implementing these techniques
  • Exploration of tantric philosophy and its relevance today
  • Guided meditation sessions
  • Interactive discussions and experience sharing
