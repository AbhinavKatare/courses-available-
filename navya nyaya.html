Navya Nyaya Course

Welcome to the Pragya Trust Navya Nyaya Course. Explore the depth of Indian logic, focusing on the intricacies of reasoning and debate.

Course Overview

Navya Nyaya, or "New Logic," is an advanced system of Indian logic and epistemology. This course provides a comprehensive study of its principles and applications.

Course Features

  • Detailed study of Navya Nyaya concepts and terminology
  • Analysis of classical texts and commentaries
  • Practical exercises in logical reasoning and debate
  • Comparative study with Western logic systems
  • Guest lectures by renowned scholars
