Puja-Path

Explore the sacred practice of ritual worship

What is Puja-Path?

Puja-Path involves ritual worship and recitation of prayers or scriptures. It is a fundamental practice in many spiritual traditions, focusing on devotion, respect, and the honoring of deities.

Benefits of Puja-Path

  • Deepens spiritual connection
  • Cultivates devotion and reverence
  • Promotes inner peace and harmony
  • Enhances focus and concentration

Common Puja-Path Practices

Aarti

Ritual of worship with light

Mantra Chanting

Recitation of sacred sounds

Offering Flowers

Presenting flowers to deities
