Reading Program

Welcome to the Pragya Trust Reading Program. Here, we focus on enhancing literacy and fostering a love for reading among our participants.

Our Approach

Our reading program is designed to cater to various age groups and reading levels. We use a combination of traditional and modern techniques to make reading engaging and enjoyable.

Program Features

  • Personalized reading plans
  • Interactive storytelling sessions
  • Comprehension exercises
  • Access to a diverse library of books
  • Regular progress assessments
